RecipeBook is a versatile theme designed for food-related content creators. It captures the charm of old-school cookbooks with its bold font choices and retro images. RecipeBook the perfect medium to share your love of cooking with the world.

Theme configuration

RecipeBook comes with a custom homepage design featuring an intro section, a blog, and a section about the author. The blog section will be populated with your latest posts automatically. You can edit the information on the homepage via the Site Editor, accessible from your dashboard, via Appearance > Editor.
